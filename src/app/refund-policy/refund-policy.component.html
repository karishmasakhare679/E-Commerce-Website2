Refund Policy

Refund against Cancellation:

1. Customers shall get Refund against Cancellation of order or any failed payment by way of credit to the original mode of payment - Credit/Debit Card or UPI or Net Banking or Third Party Wallet.
2. No refund applicable for “No returns applicable products”, if denied at doorstep for prepaid order.
3. 0/- is applicable when customers return the product. The refund amount paid to the customer will have a deduction of Rs. 30/- as a return fee. However, replacement of the product and exchange of the product is provided free of cost.
